Common questions

Everything you need to know.

What is TrustCOA?

TrustCOA is a hosted COA verification platform for peptide and supplement brands. We give you a public /verify page on your own domain where buyers can look up any batch number and see the lab results — HPLC purity, mass-spec identity, endotoxin — displayed clean, branded to you.

How does the /verify page work?

You upload your COA files and batch records to your TrustCOA dashboard. We host a public search page at yourbrand.com/verify (or a TrustCOA subdomain). A buyer types a batch number — or scans the QR code printed on your vial — and your lab results appear instantly. No DMs, no Google Drive links, no CS ticket.

What does three-lab cross-verification mean?

Three-lab cross-verification means displaying COA data from three independent laboratories side by side for the same lot. When all three confirm identity and purity independently, the page shows a 3-of-3 match badge. If labs disagree, the discrepancy is surfaced transparently. TrustCOA displays your lab data — we are the display layer, not the laboratory.

How much does TrustCOA cost?

Founder pricing starts at $97/month for the first 100 brands. This tier includes unlimited COA batches, QR code per batch, your domain and branding, and no setup fee. Founder pricing is locked for the lifetime of your active subscription.

Does TrustCOA test or authenticate my compounds?

No. TrustCOA is a display and hosting layer. We host and display the COA files you upload from your own accredited laboratories. We do not perform analytical testing, certify lab credentials, or guarantee the accuracy of the underlying lab data. The accuracy and authenticity of laboratory data is the responsibility of the brand displaying it.

How long does setup take?

Our median setup time is 18.5 hours from booking to live page. We build a skinned mock of your /verify page within 24 hours of your discovery call so you can see exactly what it will look like before committing.

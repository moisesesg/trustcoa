Privacy Policy

Last updated 2026-05-27

1. Who we are

TrustCOA is a service offered by Smart Services LLC ("we", "us"). We host certificate-of-analysis (COA) display pages on your behalf so your customers can verify lab results. We are NOT a laboratory and do not perform analytical testing.

2. What we collect

When you book a discovery call or apply for service, we collect: your name, business name, email, phone (if you provide it), the number of COAs you maintain, your current verification setup, and your preferred contact method. These are stored in our CRM (GoHighLevel) for the sole purpose of providing the service you requested.

When your customers visit a /verify page we host for you, we may log anonymous access metadata (IP, user agent, timestamp) for security and abuse-prevention purposes. We do NOT track individual buyer identities and do not use this data for advertising.

3. What we don't collect

We do NOT collect: health information about end consumers, financial account numbers (payments are processed by Stripe directly), social security numbers, government IDs, or biometric data. We don't sell or share data with third parties for advertising. We don't use behavioral tracking or fingerprinting.

4. How long we keep it

Discovery-call lead data: retained while you are an active prospect or customer. Closed/lost leads purged after 24 months. COAs you upload remain under your control and are deleted within 30 days of subscription cancellation.

5. Your rights

You can request a copy of your data, correct it, or delete it at any time by emailing us. We respond to verified requests within 30 days. California, EU, and UK residents have additional rights under CCPA / GDPR / UK-GDPR; we honor those on request.

6. Security

Your COA files are stored on Cloudflare/GoHighLevel CDN infrastructure with TLS-in-transit. Access to the admin dashboard is gated behind authenticated accounts. We do not store payment details — Stripe handles those directly.

7. Cookies

The TrustCOA marketing site uses minimal cookies for session state and analytics. /verify pages we host for you use only the cookies you choose to enable on those pages.

8. Changes to this policy

We'll post material changes on this page with a new "Last updated" date and email active customers. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: book a discovery call at trustcoa.com, call (251) 387-7712, or write to Smart Services LLC. We aim to respond within 5 business days.

TrustCOA is a digital infrastructure service. We host COA display pages provided by you. We are not a laboratory; we do not authenticate, validate, or interpret laboratory analyses. The accuracy and authenticity of the underlying laboratory data is the responsibility of the brand displaying it. Display services do not constitute medical, regulatory, or legal advice.
